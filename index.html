Reinforcement Learning Snake Game

This project is an AI-powered version of the classic Snake game. The AI uses reinforcement learning to optimize its movement strategy, learning over time how to survive longer and maximize its score. By implementing Deep Q-Learning (DQN), the model continuously improves its decision-making, showcasing the power of machine learning in gameplay optimization.

Heart Disease Prediction AI Model

Technologies: Python, Machine Learning, Decision Trees, Data Analysis

This project involves predictive modeling for heart disease diagnosis using machine learning. By analyzing a dataset containing patient health records, I applied Decision Tree Classification to identify patterns and risk factors associated with heart disease. The model provides valuable insights into how different health metrics contribute to disease prediction, demonstrating the real-world impact of AI in healthcare.

CIB Business Website

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Web Development

The Computing in Business project was my final project of my first year at Bournemouth University. It involved producing a mock up of an Incident Management System over a course of 2 weeks for a fictional company called DWK plc, which was modeled after JPMorgan and used real JPMorgan staff as our clients. We completed interviews with clients and produced detailed requirements lists then produced a database ERD diagram, mock database to demonstrate with, UI made in HTML using Figma, a risk-assessment covering cyber security threats, a sustainability report in line with the UN sustainability development goals, and a final 10 minute video demonstration of our product. At the end we displayed our final product in an expo with various different companies attending including JPMorgan and BEA Systems.
